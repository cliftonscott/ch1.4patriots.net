Q: Is it safe to buy online?

A: Buying online is absolutely one of the safest ways to shop! SSL Certificates authenticate our identity, and encrypt the information visitors enter on our site. This keeps thieves from "overhearing" any exchange between our Web page and another computer. When an SSL Certificate is installed, you can rest assured that the information you send is secured and can't be viewed by cyber crooks.

Our SSL certificate enables the browser and Web server to build a secure, encrypted connection. The SSL "handshake" process, which establishes the secure session, takes place discreetly behind the scenes without interrupting your shopping experience. A padlock icon in the browser's status bar and the "https://" prefix in the URL are the only visible indications of a secure session in progress.

The Food4Patriots checkout page is secured with a Symantec Class 3 EV SSL Web Server Certificate. Transactions on the site are protected with up to 256-bit Secure Sockets Layer encryption. Encryption means that all data transmitted is scrambled so even if something went awry, it would be meaningless to any unintended receiver.

We use a third-party credit card processor that meets the strictest international standards for data security to manage our credit card payments, so no one ever has access to your credit card information. We do NOT keep your credit card number on file.

Q. Some of your food contains Autolyzed Yeast Extract. I’ve heard this is the same as MSG.

A. Autolyzed yeast consists of concentrations of yeast cells that have been allowed to break up so that the yeasts’ own digestive enzymes break down their proteins into simpler compounds, essentially turning the yeast into a pre-digested protein. When added to food, it is considered a nutritional supplement. While it is technically possible to chemically create MSG from the various components of autolyzed yeast, this is not a normal procedure because there are much easier and simpler ways of creating MSG for commercial use. None of our food has been exposed to radiation or contains any added MSG, hydrogenated oils, trans fats, or ingredients that have been genetically modified. While salt (sodium) is a natural preservative and is found in ALL survival food on the market, we pride ourselves on having some of the lowest sodium levels in the industry.

Q. What is the shelf life of Food4Patriots?

A. Dehydrated food, stored properly (airtight and at room temperature or below) never really expires, but after 25 years the nutritional value will slowly start to decline. For that reason we guarantee a 25 year shelf life.

Q. What is the calorie count per day?

A. Our kits are based around servings. Instead of laying out meal plans for people we allow you to consume as much or as little of the product on a daily basis that meets their individual needs. The time frames (72 hours, 4 weeks, 3 months) is just a standard guideline and the food may last you a longer or shorter period of time depending upon their consumption and what other foods you may be supplementing into your daily diet from the regular pantry.

Because our food doesn't contain unnecessary fillers or high sugar contents, the calorie counts are inherently less than you will find in most store-bought food. The difference is that you are enjoying wholesome ingredients that your body recognizes and processes as “real food.”
